Instructions

On the screen you will see a series of emojis, each one hides a fruit

  • Take a guess and select 2 of these emojis, your mission is to find the right pairs
  • If you make a mistake, you'll hear an error sound
  • But if you manage to complete all the pairs, you will hear a victory sound, and find a cute surprise!!

Good luck!!!!
